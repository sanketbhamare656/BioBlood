💡 Important Blood Facts

🔄
Blood regenerates every 56 days. Regular donation is safe and healthy.
⏱️
One blood donation can save up to 3 lives through different components.
🎯
Knowing your blood type is crucial for emergencies and medical treatments.
🔬
Blood type is determined by genetics and remains constant throughout life.
